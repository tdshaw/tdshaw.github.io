Created by: Ivan Gonzales, Lyle Gonzales, Elizabeth Ochoa, Tucker Shaw, Olivia Sheehan, and Jake Tucker

Data Science!

This was our midterm project for Data Science.
We analyzed and gathered data from a database of stories submitted to the National UFO Reporting Center.
Each group picked a fun topic to present on and our group decided to focus on the stigma surrounding UFO reports.
In particular, I focused on gathering sentiment analysis (aka emotions in text).
